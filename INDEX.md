### Color Palette
```
Type          | Hex Code | Usage
--------------|----------|------------------
Primary Blue  | #1E3A8A  | Main buttons, text
Dark Blue     | #0F2344  | Gradients, bg
Gold          | #F59E0B  | Accents, CTA
Light Gold    | #FCD34D  | Highlights
White         | #FFFFFF  | Base bg
Gray-900      | #111827  | Text
Gray-600      | #4B5563  | Subtle text
```

### Typography Scale
```
Size          | Font           | Weight | Usage
--------------|---------------|--------|------------------
3xl (1.875rem)| Poppins        | bold   | Main headings

xl (1.25rem)  | Poppins        | semi   | Subsection
lg (1.125rem) | Open Sans      | semi   | Labels
base (1rem)   | Open Sans      | normal | Body text
sm (0.875rem) | Open Sans      | normal | Small text
```
